Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore

Set decades before the Harry Potter series, this prequel follows Professor Albus Dumbledore as he works with Newt Scamander to stop the dark wizard Gellert Grindelwald.

Synopsis

As Grindelwald's power grows, Dumbledore entrusts Newt and his friends with a mission to confront dark forces. The fate of the wizarding and Muggle worlds lies in their hands.

Cast & Crew

  • Directed by: David Yates
  • Produced by: J.K. Rowling, David Heyman
  • Cast: Eddie Redmayne, Jude Law, Mads Mikkelsen, Katherine Waterston

Release

Released in 2022, part of the Wizarding World franchise.
